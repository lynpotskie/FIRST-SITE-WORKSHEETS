List of spells
Aberto
A spell presumably to open objects such as doors or windows.
Accio
This charm summons an object to the caster, potentially over a significant distance
Expecto Patronum
Evokes a partially-tangible positive energy force known as Patronus
Sectumsempra
A dark spell that creates a large, blood oozing gashes on the subject as if said subject had been "slashed by a sword"
Immobulus
Renders living targets immobile
Partis Temporus
Creates a temporary gap through magical barriers
Locomotor
The spell causes the named object to rise in the air and move around at thw will of the caster
